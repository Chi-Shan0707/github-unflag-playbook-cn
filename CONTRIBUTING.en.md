## How to Contribute

### 1. Submit Your Case

If you have been flagged and are willing to share your experience:

1. Copy [cases/template.md](cases/template.md)
2. Fill in your information
3. Rename the file to `<your-github-username>.md`
4. Submit a PR
5. In the PR, confirm that you consent to making this information public

You can also:
- Fill in your information in an Issue (we'll help organize it)

### 2. Join the Existence Ledger

If you just want your existence to be recorded, without writing a full case:

1. Submit an [Issue](../../issues) with the prefix `[Existence Ledger]` in the title
2. Only provide: username + approximate time + one sentence

### 3. Improve Documentation

If you find errors, outdated information, or areas for improvement in the documentation:

1. Fork this repository
2. Create a branch
3. Make your changes
4. Submit a PR

### 4. Join Discussions

Share your experience, suggestions, or questions in [Discussions](../../discussions).

## Contribution Guidelines

### Content Requirements

- **Fact-based**: All information should be based on official documentation, community cases, or genuine personal experience
- **Cite sources**: When referencing information, note the source
- **Distinguish levels**: Clearly differentiate between "officially confirmed" and "community experience"
- **No violations**: Do not teach people how to bypass rules or use black/gray-market methods

### Conduct Requirements

- **Respect others**: People who have been flagged may be experiencing anxiety and frustration — please be kind
- **Don't blame the victim**: Do not imply that "being flagged means you did something wrong"
- **Stay objective**: Even for personal experiences, try to present the facts

### Privacy Requirements

- **Don't expose others' information**: Unless you have explicit consent
- **Sanitize sensitive data**: Ticket numbers, email addresses, and other sensitive information must be anonymized
- **Right to withdraw**: Anyone can request deletion of their information at any time

- Which document is affected
- Current content
- Suggested change
- Reason or source
